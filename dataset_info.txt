INDIAN POLITICS SENTIMENT ANALYSIS DATASET
==========================================
File: indian_politics_sentiment_dataset.csv
Total Records: 80
Created: 2026-02-28

COLUMN DESCRIPTIONS
-------------------
1. party               - Political party name
2. state               - State or "National (Central)" for central govt
3. year                - Reference year of governance/assessment
4. region_type         - "State", "National", or "UT" (Union Territory)
5. administration_score  - Governance quality score (1-10)
6. education_score       - Education policy & delivery score (1-10)
7. employment_score      - Employment rate & job creation score (1-10)
8. health_score          - Healthcare access & quality score (1-10)
9. public_transport_score - Public transport infrastructure score (1-10)
10. overall_sentiment_score - Weighted average sentiment score (1-10)
11. sentiment_label     - Categorical label: Positive / Neutral / Negative
12. governance_review   - Short text review (for NLP training)

SCORING SCALE
-------------
1.0 - 3.9  : Poor
4.0 - 5.4  : Below Average
5.5 - 6.4  : Neutral / Average
6.5 - 7.4  : Good / Positive
7.5 - 8.9  : Very Good / Strongly Positive
9.0 - 10.0 : Excellent

SENTIMENT LABELS
----------------
Positive  : overall_sentiment_score >= 6.5
Neutral   : overall_sentiment_score between 5.5 and 6.4
Negative  : overall_sentiment_score < 5.5
